You gain power based on the rankers above you, at your current ladder. The rate at which you gain power is also influenced by your bias and multi. Each bias increases the power gained, by one. Each multi multiplies that score (including the Bias) by its value.
You gain points based on your current power.

power += (rank + bias - 1) * multi;
points += power;

You gain multi and bias by sacrificing all your power and/or points. The cost is based on your current ladder.
For bias you need to sacrifice your points and for multi you reset with power, but also sacrifice all your points and bias.

upgradeCost = (currentLadder + 1) ^ (level);

You can promote to the next ladder, once there a few criteria fulfilled.
  • You need to be rank 1.
  • You need to have reached 250 million points or more.
  • You need enough people at your ladder. The minimum amount to promote is 10, but it increases from 11 and onward by 1.
  • You need a big enough lead to your pursuer. This lead is generally reached after being ~30sec in front of him. (people can throw vinegar at you during this time)
  • You need to press the promote button, that will appear on the top of your screen.
Promotion leaves behind a body, that doesn't gain power or points, for the rest of the ladder to marvel at.
If you are at the last ladder with all your peers, the ability to promote turns into an Asshole-Button, which, if enough people press it:

RESETS THE GAME!

You can gain sour grapes in 2 ways.
  • Being last, gains you 1 sour grape every second.
  • Getting passed by someone else also rewards you with 1 sour grape.
Also, you gain vinegar based on your current grape-count. (if you are not #1)
This vinegar can be used to throw onto the top person, if they are rank 1. If they have more vinegar than you, you just subtract your amount of vinegar from theirs, but if you throw more than they can handle, you force them to buy a multi or reset their points to 0 if they can't afford it.

There is a way that you can auto-promote, which unlocks from Ladder 2 onward. This feature, can be bought using your hard-earned grapes and completely bypasses the criteria, that you need a big enough lead to promote.
Also, it's completely server-sided, so once you have it, no amount of vinegar can hurt you this ladder, even if you are not online. The cost of this feature gets cheaper, depending on how far behind you are on your current climb.
Sadly, this feature is not available on the last ladder, which means no one, can escape the grapes of their competitors.
